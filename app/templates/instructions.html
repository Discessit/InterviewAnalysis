How to Use Interview Analysis

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Prepare Your Video: Ensure your interview video is in MP4 format and is at least 10 minutes long for accurate analysis.
  2. Navigate to Upload Page: Click on the "Upload Video" link in the navigation bar or the button on the homepage.
  3. Upload Your Video: On the upload page, click "Select Video" to choose your MP4 file, then click "Analyze" to start the process.
  4. Wait for Analysis: The system will process your video using the Gemini API, analyzing soft skills and interview integrity. This may take a few moments.
  5. Review Results: Once complete, you'll be redirected to a results page with detailed scores for naturalness, likeability, communication, and integrity, along with a recommendation.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use a high-quality video with clear audio to ensure accurate analysis.
  • Ensure the candidate is clearly visible and facing the camera.
  • Avoid background noise or distractions in the video.
